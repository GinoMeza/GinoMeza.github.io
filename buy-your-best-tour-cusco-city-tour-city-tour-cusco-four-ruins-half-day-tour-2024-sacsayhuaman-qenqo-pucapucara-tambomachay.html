    Cusco City Tour | City Tour Cusco | Four Ruins Half-Day Tour 2024, Sacsayhuaman, Qenqo, Pucapucara, Tambomachay, Koricancha in our Exclusive Private Tour

    This nice Cusco City Tour by Sacsayhuaman, Qenqo, Pucapucara, Tambomachay, and Qoricancha in our Private Service begins with the pick-up of the customers from their hotels of Cusco city at your best time, and then we will star with the visit of the amazing Temple of the Incas Saqsayhuaman, one of the iconic buildings that leave us breathless, predominantly by huge stones ordered built by the Inca Pachacutec in the XV century, it is an immense fortress built with large stones whose transport and styled was a mystery, then we will visit Qenqo known as the Labyrinth where the Incas performed their mummification before to buried and then we’re going to Pucapucara the control place of the Incas of  Cusco and the last visit will be  Tambomachay, where the Incas performed the purification ceremonies and rituals in honor of the water, it was made for now becomes a mystery and enigma left by our ancestors the Incas, after this tour we will transfer until your same hotel in Cusco City
